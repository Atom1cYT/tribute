Nicholas Stewart better known online as Jynxzi or Junko, is an American gamer and live-streamer who lives in Florida, United States. Jynxzi predominantly plays the video game Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege, but has branched out to other games such as Five Nights at Freddy's, Fortnite, Rocket League and Only Up.
